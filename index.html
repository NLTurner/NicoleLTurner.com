About Nicole

Creating Change from the Inside Out

Nicole L. Turner is a sought-after keynote speaker, strategic advisor, and creator of the Inside-Out Transformation Framework™. She empowers individuals, leaders, and organizations to shift mindsets, strengthen culture, and achieve lasting growth from the inside out.

As the founder of Detox For Your Life¬Æ, The Culture Pro¬Æ, and the Turner Training Institute™, Nicole blends expertise in personal development, organizational strategy, and business leadership to help people think differently so they can live, lead, and build with intention.

An accomplished author of ten books spanning self-help, leadership, workplace culture, and entrepreneurship, she guides individuals toward personal transformation, equips leaders to build strong cultures, and challenges founders to think strategically as they create businesses that matter. Her work has been featured on national and international stages, and she partners with people and organizations committed to meaningful, sustainable transformation.
